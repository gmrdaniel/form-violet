## **Pricing Approach: 3 Options \+ Open Field**

Each service shows 3 suggested price points (low, mid, high) plus an 'Other: $\_\_\_' open field. This balances two needs:

**REFERENCE POINTS:** Creators who have no idea what to write see 3 concrete numbers and can pick one quickly. No blank-field paralysis.

**DISCOVERY:** The 'Other' field lets creators who would pay more (or less) than the 3 options express their true willingness. You discover the $150/mo creator who would have been capped at $99 in a fixed-option survey.

**ANCHORING CONTROL:** The 3 prices are intentionally spread wide (e.g., $29 / $59 / $99) so the midpoint doesn't dominate. The range signals that there's no 'right answer,' just preference.

## **Completion Rate Optimization Checklist**

| Tactic | Where | Why it works |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Incentive on first screen** | Welcome page, before any questions | People need a reason to start. 'Get free access when we launch' is a concrete reward. |
| **Progress \+ time estimate** | Every section: 'Section 2 of 4 \- About 2 min left' | Uncertainty causes abandonment. 4 sections removes anxiety. |
| **Social proof counter** | Welcome: '\[X\] creators have already responded' | Nobody wants to be first. Update weekly. Seed with 47 if needed. |
| **One matrix instead of 13 pages** | Section 2 | Scrolling a table is faster than tapping 13 screens. 70% less perceived effort. |
| **3 prices \+ Other (not fully open)** | Price field per service | Faster than typing from scratch. Still allows discovery. Best of both worlds. |
| **Bundle savings table shown visually** | Section 3, visible while answering | When they SEE the savings, bundles become obvious choices. |
| **Mandatory contact \+ incentive** | Section 4 | 'Say Yes for early access.' Feels like a gift, not an intrusion. |
| **Referral on thank you page** | After submission | Post-completion generosity. Perfect moment to ask for a share. |
| **WhatsApp-native delivery** | Distribution | 90%+ open on mobile via WhatsApp. Form must be thumb-friendly. |
| **Tuesday-Wednesday, 10am-12pm** | Send time | Highest open rates. Avoid weekends and Monday mornings. |
